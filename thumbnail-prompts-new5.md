# SLIDE UNIVERSE — 新規5スタイルのサムネ生成プロンプト一式

各スタイル3枚（① キービジュアル＝表紙 / ② スライド適用例 / ③ スライド適用例）。
GPT等の画像生成にそのまま貼って使用。出力は 16:9。
保存ファイル名の対応：`<id>.webp`（①）/ `<id>-1.webp`（②）/ `<id>-2.webp`（③）

## 1. Doodle Collage（doodle-collage）

### ① キービジュアル
```
16:9 landscape key visual. Vintage magazine editorial collage that looks like a "cut-and-paste notebook".
Warm cream background (#F2EEE7), generous whitespace (about 50%).
On the left: oversized elegant editorial serif title "Doodle Collage" in near-black (#111111), bold.
On the right: a bold, high-contrast black-and-white cutout portrait of an expressive person (fashion-editorial pose), partial crop, soft matte grayscale with very slight grain, cut out from its background with hard paper edges (no drop shadow).
Layered mustard (#DDBE63) geometric rectangle blocks behind the photo, overlapping rectangles, asymmetrical magazine balance.
Hand-drawn marker doodles in coral (#E56A67) used sparingly: a circle around one keyword, an underline, a small arrow, a starburst — medium stroke, slightly imperfect.
A mustard marker highlight behind a short subtitle word.
Flat, no gradients, no borders, no heavy shadows, soft paper grain. Editorial, artistic, playful, human, emotional.
```

## 5. Editorial Infographic（editorial-infographic）

### ① キービジュアル
```
16:9 landscape key visual. Scandinavian-minimal magazine editorial infographic, light and friendly.
Off-white background (#F7F7F5), generous whitespace, three-column magazine grid feel.
Large bold CONDENSED uppercase sans-serif title "EDITORIAL INFOGRAPHIC" in near-black (#111111), tight letter-spacing (-0.03em), establishing strong hierarchy.
Around it: flat-vector minimal editorial illustrations and outline pictogram icons (monitor, envelope, globe-network, eyeglasses) with rounded geometry and thin outlines — every icon and illustration casts a consistent subtle LONG SHADOW toward the bottom-right (this is the signature).
Infographic modules: a donut/ring chart, a percentage circle, a small horizontal comparison bar, a vertical timeline, a statistic callout.
Pastel accents used sparingly, max 3 colors: mint (#74D7D3), pink (#F27EB2), slate (#547283). Thin divider lines, dashed guide lines, minimal connectors.
Completely flat — no gradients, no textures, no heavy decoration. Fresh, friendly, modern, informative, playful but sophisticated.
```

Three modules across: (1) a mint (#74D7D3) donut/percentage circle with a big condensed number, (2) a pink (#F27EB2) horizontal comparison bar, (3) a slate (#547283) statistic callout with a flat outline icon.
